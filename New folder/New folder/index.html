About us!

Mitch Sweets and Pastries is your go-to spot for delicious homemade cakes and refreshing halo-halo, made with love and the finest ingredients. Each cake is baked fresh, offering the perfect balance of flavor and softness for any occasion, while our halo-halo is a delightful mix of traditional Filipino ingredients, crushed ice, and sweet toppings that bring comfort and joy in every scoop. Whether you're celebrating or simply craving something sweet, we're here to serve you treats that feel like home.
